Satyr:

	Ability Score Increase:
Your charisma score increases by 2;
Your Dexterity Increases by 1;

		Age:
Satyrs mature and age about the same rate as humans

	     *Alignment*
Satyrs delight in living a life free of the mantle of law.
They gravitate toward being good, but some
have devious streaks and enjoy dismay.

               *Size*
Satyrs range from just under 5 ft to about 6 ft in height,
generally with slender builds.
Your size is medium.
	       *Speed*
Your walking speed is 35.

               *Fey*

Your creature type is fey, rather than humanoid.
	       *Ram*
 You can use your head and horns to make unarmed strikes.
 If you hit with them, you deal bludgeoning damage equal to 
1d4 + your Strength modifier.

	   *Magical Resistance*

You have advantage on saving throws against spells 
and other magical effects.

	    *Mirthful Leaps*

Whenever you make a long or high jump, you can roll a d8 
and add the number to the number of feet you cover,
even when making a standing jump. 
This extra distance costs movement as normal.
	    *Reveler*
You have proficiency in the Performance and Persuasion skills,
 and you have proficiency with one musical instrument of your choice.

	    *Languages*
You can speak, read, and write Common and Sylvan.
